What is Splankna?

Splankna is a God-honoring, mind/body protocol for emotional healing. If traditional talk therapy enters trauma from the verbal door, Splankna accesses trauma through the body door. This is great news for children (who don't know how to communicate about their feelings), adults who don't like to talk about their feelings, and everyone in between. At the Healing House you can enter knowing that you will be seen, heard, and ushered to the feet of Jesus with a certified Splankna Practitioner at every appointment.

For more information, please visit splankna.com and check out their helpful videos.
